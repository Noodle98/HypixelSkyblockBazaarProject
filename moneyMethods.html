Forge: Gemstone Mixture

Requirements:

  • Skyblock level 7 (for bazaar access)
  • Dwarven Mines unlocked
  • Heart of the Mountain Tier 4

Effort level: Low

Gemstone Mixture is a Forge item, forged at Forge Basin in the Dwarven Mines.
You start with 4 forging slots, but this can be increased to 5 at /hotm tier 5
By default, Gemstone Mixtures take 4 hours to forge, however his can be decreased by
upgrading the "Quick Forge" perk in /hotm (unlocked at hotm tier 2).
I highly recommend getting the travel scroll to the forge, called:
"Travel Scroll to the Dwarven Forge", to make forging these more seemless.
You can then just type "/warpforge" (without quotations) in chat.

To make 1x Gemstone Mixture, you need to buy order the following materials at the bazaar:

  • 4x Fine Jade Gemstone
  • 4x Fine Amber Gemstone
  • 4x Fine Sapphire Gemstone
  • 4x Fine Amethyst Gemstone
  • 320x Sludge Juice

Input the amount of Gemstone Mixtures you want to craft
In the input field below, and click "Calculate".

Enchanted Lava Bucket

  • Skyblock level 7 (for bazaar access)
  • Coal collection level VIII (8)
  • Iron collection level IV (4)

Effort level: Low

Enchanted Lava Bucket is a minion fuel that gives +25% minion speed.
It is unlocked from Coal collection lvl VIII (8), which can be achieved easily,
By placing coal minions on your island. Alternatively, you can mine it manually in the Gold Mine.
The reason we additonally want Iron collection level IV (4), is to make even more profit,
By buy ordering regular iron ingots and turning them into the 3 enchanted, needed for the bucket.
I highly recommend getting a personal compactor 4000 or higher, to turn the iron into enchanted iron automatically.

Although you might have noticed that the profit per Enchanted Lava Bucket is low, don't skip this method just yet.
The real money potential from this method comes from the fact that with just 1 buy order for each material type,
You can make many buckets at once. Also the buy orders usually fill very fast,
Making this method very easy, and very effecient.
If you invest more money into materials, you can make a lot more money in about the same amount of time.

To make 1x Enchanted Lava Bucket, you need to buy order the following materials:

  • 2x Enchanted Coal Block
  • 480x Iron Ingot

Input the amount of Enchanted Lava Buckets you want to craft,
In the input field below, and click "Calculate".
