Bazaar Price Checker Money Making Methods

Money making methods using bazaar items

Forge: Gemstone Mixture

Prerequisites:

  • Dwarven Mines unlocked
  • Heart of the Mountain Tier 4

Effort level: Low

Gemstone Mixture is a Forge item, forged at Forge Basin in the Dwarven Mines.
I highly recommend getting the travel scroll to the forge, called:
"Travel Scroll to the Dwarven Forge", to make forging these more seemless.
You can then just type "/warpforge" (without quotations) in chat.

To make 1x Gemstone Mixture, you need to buy order the following materials at the bazaar:

  • 4x Fine Jade Gemstone
  • 4x Fine Amber Gemstone
  • 4x Fine Sapphire Gemstone
  • 4x Fine Amethyst Gemstone
  • 320x Sludge Juice

Input the amount of Gemstone Mixtures you want to craft in the input field below, and click "Calculate".
